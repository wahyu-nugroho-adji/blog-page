Ketoprak

    Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Rice noodles
  • Tofu
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Sauce

    Instructions:

  1. Mix the meat with flour
  2. Make it like a small ball
  3. Boil a water
  4. Put the meatball to boiled water
  5. Put the tofu to boiled water
  6. Put the rice noodles to boiled water
